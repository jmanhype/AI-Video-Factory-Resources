# Weekly Rituals – AI Video Factory

## PROOF WEDNESDAY

**Time**: All day Wednesday
**Format**: Post exactly three things

1. Before → After (image or 10-sec clip)
2. Your prompt (copy/paste)
3. One lesson you learned

**Rule**: Reply to one other post with a concrete tip.

Low-effort posts get removed. Build signal, not noise.

---

## BUILD FRIDAY

**Time**: Every Friday, 10:00am Central (live session)
**Duration**: 10 minutes, start to posted

**Bring**:
- Your track (Face / B-Roll / Product-Safe / Voiceover / Explainer)
- A one-line outcome
- Rights-cleared assets (or stock link)

**Flow**:
1. Inputs → prompt → render
2. Export → post live
3. Link your post in comments for quick fixes

No perfection. Just ship.

---

## OFFER SUNDAY

**Time**: All day Sunday
**Format**: One post, one link

**Include**:
- Who it's for
- Outcome in one line
- What's inside (3 bullets max)
- Link

**Rules**:
- No DMs in comments
- Be clear; be brief
- Posts that don't follow format get removed
